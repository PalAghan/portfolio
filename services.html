Questions?

Things people usually ask before we get started.

How long does a website project take? +

A standard 5-page portfolio or landing page typically takes 1–3 weeks depending on complexity and how quickly we align on the design. I'll always give you a realistic timeline upfront before we start so there are no surprises.

How much do you charge? +

Pricing depends on the scope of the project — a simple landing page is priced differently from a multi-page site with custom animations. Reach out via the contact page and I'll give you a clear quote based on what you actually need.

What do you need from me to get started? +

Just a clear idea of what you want — your content (text, images, logo), any design references you like, and your target audience. If you don't have a design yet, I can help with that too. We figure out the rest together.

Do you work with clients outside Nairobi? +

Absolutely. Frontend development and UI/UX design are fully remote services — I can work with anyone anywhere. Electronics and delivery is currently Nairobi-based only, but reach out and we can figure something out.
